Natu Kodi Pulusu: A Spicy Andhra Delicacy!

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole free-range chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 3 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tbsp red chilli powder
  • 1 tbsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • 1/2 cup tamarind extract
  • 1/4 cup sesame oil
  • 2 cups water
  • salt to taste

Recipe:

  1. Marinate Chicken: Marinate chicken pieces with turmeric powder, salt, and ginger-garlic paste. Let it sit for 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare Masala: Heat oil in a pan, add fenugreek seeds, curry leaves, and chopped onions. Saute until onions turn golden brown.
  3. Cook Chicken: Add tomatoes, red chilli powder, coriander powder, and cook until tomatoes are mushy. Add marinated chicken and saute for 5 minutes.
  4. Add Tamarind Extract: Pour tamarind extract and water. Cover and cook on low heat until chicken is tender and the gravy thickens.
  5. Serve: Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with steamed rice or roti. Enjoy the spicy flavors of Natu Kodi Pulusu!
