Apricot Delight: Sweet Bliss in Every Bite!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups dried apricots
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 cup almonds, blanched and sliced
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup rose water (optional)
  • 1/2 cup cream or ice cream (for serving)

Recipe:

  1. Prepare Apricots: Wash apricots and soak them in water overnight or for at least 6 hours until they are soft.
  2. Cook Apricots: In a saucepan, cook apricots with sugar, water, and cardamom powder until apricots are tender and the mixture thickens.
  3. Blend and Chill: Let the mixture cool, then blend until smooth. Add lemon juice and rose water if using. Chill in the refrigerator for a few hours.
  4. Serve: Serve chilled apricot delight topped with sliced almonds and a dollop of cream or ice cream. Enjoy the delightful sweetness!
