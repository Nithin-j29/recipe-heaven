Hyderabadi Chicken Biryani: A Royal Feast!

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup yoghurt (curd)
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup coriander leaves
  • 4 green chillies, slit
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp biryani masala
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 4 cups water
  • salt to taste

Recipe:

  1. Marinate Chicken: Marinate chicken with yoghurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, and salt. Let it sit for 30 minutes.
  2. Cook Rice: Parboil rice with whole spices until 70% cooked. Drain and set aside.
  3. Layer and Dum: Layer cooked chicken, rice, fried onions, mint, coriander leaves in a heavy-bottomed pan. Sprinkle biryani masala and ghee. Seal with foil and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes.
  4. Serve: Serve hot with raita and salad. Enjoy the flavors of this aromatic biryani!
